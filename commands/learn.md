## Getting Started

1. Choose a path that aligns with your interests
2. Use `/browse-agent` to explore specific agents
3. Use `/assess` to evaluate your current knowledge
4. Follow the recommended roadmap and resources
5. Build projects to reinforce learning

## Tips for Success

- **Consistency**: Dedicate regular time to learning
- **Practice**: Build projects alongside theory
- **Community**: Join developer communities
- **Feedback**: Seek code reviews and mentorship
- **Reflection**: Regularly assess your progress
